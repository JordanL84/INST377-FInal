About the Project

Helping users avoid unreliable health searches

This app helps people find simple, trustworthy health information from MedlinePlus instead of random websites.

The Problem

Many people search symptoms online and find confusing, exaggerated, or unreliable information. That can increase anxiety and make it harder to understand basic health topics.

Stakeholders

The main stakeholders are everyday internet users who want understandable health information. A secondary stakeholder group is public health educators who care about people finding reliable sources.

Data Source

This app uses the MedlinePlus Health Topics search API. MedlinePlus is produced by the National Library of Medicine and provides health information for public education.
