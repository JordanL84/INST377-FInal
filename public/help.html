How to Use the App

Use this app as a starting point for learning about health topics.

1

Enter one symptom, condition, or topic in the search box.

2

Review the MedlinePlus results returned by the app.

3

Click “Learn More” to read the full official MedlinePlus page.

Tips

  • Search one topic at a time, like “headache” instead of a full sentence.
  • Use this for learning, not for diagnosis.
  • Contact a doctor or emergency service for serious or urgent symptoms.
